# Monetization Strategy - FastLane

## Packaging
### Free
- Timer basics
- Up to 14 days history
- 1 reminder channel

### Pro
- Unlimited history
- Advanced insights and trend cards
- Smart reminders and routines
- Premium fasting protocols and coaching prompts

## Paywall Moments
1. Post-onboarding soft paywall.
2. After first meaningful usage milestone.
3. At locked premium insight cards.

## Pricing Defaults
- Monthly: $9.99
- Yearly: $59.99 (best value anchor)

## Conversion Principles
- Clear value before ask.
- Honest feature differentiation.
- One-tap close for trust.
- No deceptive countdowns or dark patterns.

## Production Follow-Up
- Stripe Checkout sessions.
- Billing portal for self-service cancel/manage.
- Webhook-driven entitlement updates.
