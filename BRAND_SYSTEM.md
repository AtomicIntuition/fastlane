# FastLane Brand System

## Positioning
FastLane is the premium, no-noise fasting app for people who want consistency and clarity without gimmicks.

## Tone of Voice
- Confident, calm, evidence-aware.
- Encouraging without hype.
- Direct and practical.

## Visual Direction
- Theme: dawn-to-dusk metabolic rhythm.
- Style: editorial + performance dashboard hybrid.
- Texture: layered gradients and subtle grid overlays.

## Color Tokens
- `--fl-bg-1`: #0f172a
- `--fl-bg-2`: #1e293b
- `--fl-accent`: #22c55e
- `--fl-accent-2`: #f59e0b
- `--fl-ink`: #e2e8f0
- `--fl-muted`: #94a3b8
- `--fl-card`: rgba(15, 23, 42, 0.62)

## Typography
- Display: Fraunces (headlines)
- UI: Space Grotesk (body/navigation)

## UX Rules
- Every screen has one primary action.
- Core action always thumb-accessible on mobile.
- High contrast for legibility in bright and dim environments.
- Motion is meaningful (progress, transitions, celebration).
